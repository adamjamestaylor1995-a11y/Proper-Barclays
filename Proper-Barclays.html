How to Play
Relive the greatest era of Premier League football and its iconic players.

Create your own club, hire a manager, pick your formation, and draft 7 players who can lead your team to glory.

Spend your budget wisely - run out of funds and you'll need to resort to free agents!

Strategy and a bit of luck will be crucial if you want to climb up the divisions and win silverware.
